# Support BraceKit

BraceKit is a free Chrome extension built and maintained independently. If it has been useful to you, consider supporting its development.

## Why Support?

BraceKit is developed in spare time with no corporate backing. Your support helps cover:

- **Infrastructure costs** — Domain hosting and documentation server.
- **Development time** — Building new features, fixing bugs, and keeping up with browser API changes.
- **MCP & provider integrations** — Testing and maintaining support for multiple AI providers and MCP servers.
- **Long-term sustainability** — Keeping BraceKit free, and ad-free for everyone.

Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a real difference.

## How to Support

### Ko-fi

Ko-fi is a simple, no-account-required way to send a one-time or recurring contribution.

**[Support on Ko-fi &rarr;](https://ko-fi.com/bracekit)**

Ko-fi accepts **credit/debit cards** and **PayPal**.

---

### Saweria

Saweria is a local Indonesian platform, ideal if you prefer to pay via local bank transfer, e-wallet (GoPay, OVO, DANA, ShopeePay), or QRIS.

**[Support on Saweria &rarr;](https://saweria.co/bracekit)**

Saweria accepts:
- QRIS (all e-wallets & mobile banking)
- Bank transfer (BCA, Mandiri, BNI, BRI, etc.)
- GoPay, OVO, DANA, ShopeePay

## Other Ways to Help

Not in a position to donate financially? That's completely fine. There are other valuable ways to support the project:

- **[Report bugs](https://github.com/nexifle/brace-kit/issues)** — Open an issue if you find something broken.
- **Spread the word** — Tell a friend or colleague who might find BraceKit useful.

## Transparency

Donations go directly to the BraceKit maintainer. They are used to cover operational costs first, with any remainder reinvested into development time.

BraceKit will remain free regardless of donation volume. Supporters do not receive special features or early access — this is simply a way to say thank you.
